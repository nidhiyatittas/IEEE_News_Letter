Welcome to IEEE LaunchPad Kerala!

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala is a vibrant platform dedicated to fostering innovation and collaboration among technology enthusiasts, professionals, and students in Kerala. Our mission is to provide a launchpad for ideas, projects, and initiatives that drive technological advancement and community growth.

Join Us Today!

We invite you to join IEEE LaunchPad Kerala and be a part of our exciting journey. Participate in our events, contribute to projects, and connect with like-minded individuals. Together, we can make a difference and shape the future of technology in Kerala.

Event Roadmap

Step 1: Online Registration in Launchpad Website

The candidate must register through a registration form to complete the application for Launchpad Kerala 2024.

Step 2: Confirmation

Candidates will receive an email from the Launchpad Kerala 2024 team within 48 hours of registration. The next steps will be communicated either through email or text messages. All updates will also be posted on the updates page of the Launchpad Kerala 2024 website.

Step 3: Join the Community

Join the GTech MuLearn Discord Server and complete the Launchpad Challenges. You can find the details here: GTech MuLearn GitHub.

Step 4: Technical Challenge

Once logged into the MuLearn Discord Server, you can find the levels and tasks related to the IEEE Launchpad category in the channel #ieee-launchpad-announcement:

  • Level 1: Foundational Skills and Introduction
  • Level 2: Specialized Learning Paths and Challenges
  • Level 3: Industry-curated Challenges and Hackathons
Submit your completed tasks on time and avail Karma points.

Step 5: Online Aptitude Test

Put your abilities to the test in our online assessment, where you'll showcase your competencies to potential employers.

Step 6: Await the Finalized Results

Stay tuned for the announcement of the finalized results. Your performance in both the Technical Challenge and the online test will be thoroughly evaluated, unlocking exciting opportunities based on your achievements.

Step 7: Final Interview

As the final stage of the selection process, gear up for the interview. This is your ultimate opportunity to showcase your skills and leave a lasting impression on recruiters. It's the final step towards securing your dream job.
